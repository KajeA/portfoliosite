About Me

I have always been fascinated with technology and computers, from the days of DOS to the current era of VR, AI and RGB. I love playing and trying to break games and figuring out exactly how things work, or why they don’t.

So far, my professional life has been a whirlwind of loud music, tours, strange rider requests and writing about people’s idols. It’s usually a nonstop rush of fun and chaos, however, the music industry isn’t the final destination for me; I’ve been working dutifully to make the career switch into software development. Ideally, I want to work in an air conditioned office with endless problems to solve. Endless snacks would also be cool.

I relish the small victories in life, like finally getting that piece of code to work or lighting a bonfire in Dark Souls. I have an abundance of patience and my favourite part of development is the creative problem solving involved and how there are multiple solutions to every problem.
